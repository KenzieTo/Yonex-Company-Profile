About us

We are a great team of four Kenzie, Jericco, Joshua, and Kevin, proudly based in Japan. United by our passion for badminton, we strive to provide top-quality equipment for players of all levels. With diverse expertise and a shared commitment to excellence, we aim to support and elevate your game with gear that delivers both performance and reliability.

Why Choose Us?

We believe that badminton is more than just a sport; it's a powerful way to build connections, foster camaraderie, and cultivate a strong competitive spirit, all while promoting a healthy and active lifestyle. As passionate players and dedicated fans, we understand the dedication and quality needed to help you perform at your best on the court.

What We Offer?

We provide a wide range of badminton equipment, from rackets, shuttlecocks, shoes, to other supporting accessories. Every product we sell has been carefully selected to ensure you get the best.Based in Japan, we're committed to delivering world-class products and exceptional service. Join us on this journey and make every badminton game more meaningful with our high-quality equipment!
